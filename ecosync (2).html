01About EcoSync

Building the future of climate intelligence

EcoSync exists because climate performance is still managed on spreadsheets and good intentions. We give Indian businesses the same rigour for carbon that they already demand for capital.

Mission

Make carbon legible

Turn every tonne a business emits into a number it can see, audit, and act on — without hiring a climate department.

Vision

Climate as a balance sheet

A market where climate performance is reported, priced, and rewarded with the same discipline as financial performance.

Why EcoSync

Advisory meets exchange

Most firms measure or trade. We do both — so the same data that builds your emission report also executes your offsets.

Phase 01

Carbon accounting and audit engagements — measurement first, always.

Phase 02

Exchange goes live

A verified carbon credit marketplace opens to advisory clients.

Phase 03

Integrated platform

02The problem

Four gaps. One missing platform.

P·01

Footprints are invisible

Most businesses don't know their carbon footprint — Scope 1, 2, and 3 sit unmeasured across fuel bills, power meters, and supply chains.

P·02

Markets are fragmented

Carbon credits trade across scattered registries, brokers, and standards — no single trusted venue, no transparent price.

P·03

ESG is now mandatory

BRSR and global disclosure regimes are turning sustainability reporting from a brochure into a compliance document with teeth.

P·04

MSMEs are priced out

Climate expertise is sold at enterprise retainers. India's 63 million MSMEs can't afford consultants — so they do nothing.

03The solution

One platform. Two verticals.

Advisory measures and manages. Exchange offsets and proves. The same ledger runs both — so nothing is claimed that wasn't first counted.
